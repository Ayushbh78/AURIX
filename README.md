# Ayush's Personal Productivity Tracker

A complete dark-themed personal life management system built with Python Flask.

## Features
- **Dashboard** — Daily productivity score, habits, spending, study, goals overview
- **Habits** — Daily habit tracking with streaks, history, weekly progress
- **Daily Planner** — One-time tasks with priorities, deadlines, overdue detection
- **Spending Tracker** — Full expense log, budget tracking, category breakdown, charts
- **Study + Exam Tracker** — Exam-based (Exam → Subject → Chapter) & Independent subjects, revision log, difficulty tracking, hour estimation
- **Calendar** — Monthly view with productivity color coding, click any day for full breakdown
- **Notes** — Date-based notes with categories and pinning
- **Life Goals** — Long-term goals with milestones and progress tracking
- **Sleep Tracker** — Log sleep/wake time, quality, 14-day chart
- **Reports & Analytics** — 7-day productivity, habit consistency, spending breakdown, sleep trend
- **Focus Sessions** — Pomodoro-style timer with auto-save
- **Mood & Water Tracking** — Daily mood emoji and water intake logging
